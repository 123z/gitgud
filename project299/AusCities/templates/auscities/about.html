About

SmartCity can be used to find out information about any given city. Starting out with Brisbane city, users can register accounts as Students, Tourists or Businessmen and view information about the city to help plan journeys, and ensure that any trip to a new city is well planned for. The user can look at information about:

  • Libraries
  • Colleges
  • Hotels
  • Industries
  • Parks
  • Zoos
  • Museums
  • Restaurants
  • Malls
